About

Aaliyah Felicity Jasmin Sanchez, is a 20-year-old, 2nd year Computer Science Student at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa.

Was born on September 21, 2002, at Las Pinas City. She has been living at Las Pinas City for 20 years since she was born.
She lives at P. Mapa street, DBP Village, Almanza Dos, Las Pinas City.

Her parents are Juliet J. Sanchez, 45 years of age, a Overseas Filipino Worker at Kuwait and Paul Hernand M. Sanchez, 45 years of age, a Systems Administrator at Cantier.
She has 2 siblings, next to her is Aamir Felipe Sanchez, 14 years old, a Grade 9 student and Aairah Franchesca J. Sanchez, her youngest sibling, 9 years old, a Grade 4 student.

In preschool, she studied at Teacher Aida's Learning Center.

In primary school, she studied at the following schools: STI Academy, Blessed Trinity School, and Almanza Elementary School.

For Junior High School, she studied at Las Pinas City National High School - Almanza.

For Senior High School, she took up Technical Vocational Livelihood - Information and Communications Technology at Las Pinas City National Senior High School - Dona Josefa Campus.

As of the moment, she is a 2nd year student, taking up Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa.

5 years from now, she said that she sees herself as a successful woman on her chosen career.
